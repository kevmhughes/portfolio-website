About

I'm a web developer based in Malaga with a passion for building clean, functional, and beautifully designed applications. I enjoy turning complex ideas into seamless digital experiences, always with a focus on user-centered design and high-quality code.

I'm currently open to both full-time and freelance opportunities. My experience has taught me how to balance creativity with technical precision, delivering solutions that not only meet requirements but also delight users and clients alike.

Beyond coding, I draw inspiration from my other passions: exploring global coffee varieties, music production and collecting records from an array of musical styles, and experimenting with international cuisine at home. These interests fuel my creativity and help me bring fresh perspectives into my work.
