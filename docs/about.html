About Me

Hi, I'm Kevin Hughes, a passionate web developer based in Barcelona, driven by a love for beautifully designed applications and a commitment to clean, flawless code. I am currently open to full-time and freelance opportunities. My experience in past roles has sharpened my client-focused approach, enabling me to exceed stakeholder expectations and consistently deliver high-quality work on time, tailored precisely to client needs.

My goal is to join an energetic, innovative team that pushes the boundaries of digital experiences, creating inspiring applications that go beyond user expectations. I'm eager to collaborate with like-minded professionals who value creativity, technical excellence, and user-centered design. I believe that the best applications not only function seamlessly but also bring delight to their users and make a lasting impact. By contributing my skills in front-end and back-end development, along with a meticulous attention to detail, I aim to enhance every project I work on in turn leaving users and stakeholders truly impressed and thoroughly satisfied.

When I am not coding, I love to check out freshly ground coffee from all around the world. In fact my favourite type is the Red Caturra bean from Rwanda. I even had the chance to try the world's most expensive coffee, kopi luwak, in Indonesia, but I'll leave out the details on how it's fermented!

I get inspiration from listening to my collection of Jazz, Funk, and Disco records, DJing at Jazz festivals, and producing music. I love music technology in general, and I have lots of equipment such as a Moog synthesiser, a sampler, and other fantastic midi tools.
